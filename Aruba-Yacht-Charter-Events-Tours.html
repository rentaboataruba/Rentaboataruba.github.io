We Provide an Exclusive Aruba Yacht Charter Experience

Do you want to spend a memorable day at sea? Discover Aruba from the water, and make this the highlight of your Aruba vacation! Exclusive Boat Aruba VIP offers private tours, fishing charters, cruises, and special occasions or events on the island of Aruba, delivering the ultimate experience on the water aboard a private yacht.

Hourly or Daily Tours

All along the leeward coast of Aruba, the calm, flat sea and the steady trade winds make sailing an ideal activity to add to your holiday experiences.

Set sail for a glorious cruise along Aruba’s scenic coastline. One of the most breathtaking perspectives of our island is the view of our stunning beaches from the sea as you sail along our coastline.

Private Yacht Charter Events

Charter an Exclusive Boat Aruba yacht for the ultimate celebration or special event. We specialize in private boat tours, weddings, holiday events, birthday parties, corporate events, and more!

Relax and enjoy life for a few hours and experience a beautiful sunrise or a sunset party on a boat, where you can booze freely and dance to the loud music as we cruise along.

Private Fishing Charter

Enjoy an exclusive deep-sea fishing experience located on the beautiful island of Aruba. We offer open-boat offshore fishing trips with the friendliest crew and the best value for money.

Ready for action, adventure, and the experience of a lifetime? Come and join us on one of our exclusive fishing trips.

Yacht Catering Services

We offer excellent catering options for all of our charters or events, including a private chef and everything you need to enjoy a day on the water.

Our experience in organizing private boat charters guarantees the best catering and ensures you and your party have an unforgettable time with loads of fun!
