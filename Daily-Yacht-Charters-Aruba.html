Daily Yacht Charter Detail Information

If you don’t want to spend the whole day onboard your chartered yacht, you can opt for something different and rent an hourly boat tour. The minimum rental period is generally 2 hours. Our daily boat tours depart from Varadero Aruba Marina & Boatyard in the morning and last until the evening. If you have any questions or issues about your charter, please contact our reservations team.
