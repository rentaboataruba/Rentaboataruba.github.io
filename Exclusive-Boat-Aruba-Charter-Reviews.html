We Look Forward to Welcoming You Aboard!

You can go on an exclusive boat trip in Aruba with your loved ones and celebrate many special days, such as birthdays, anniversaries, and honeymoons. For this reason, by renting a boat from Exclusive Boat Aruba, you will have the opportunity to celebrate this special day on a tropical island. We offer tailor-made yacht and fishing charters in Aruba for private parties to make sure that your event is personalized to your individual needs and goes as smoothly as possible.

The price of a private charter depends on several factors, like: the day of the week, the number of guests, the catering, and high and low season. Please e-mail us with your wishes, so we can make you a suitable offer!
