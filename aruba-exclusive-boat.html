Boat rentals & charters in Aruba

Welcome to Exclusive Boat Aruba, We invite you to cruise with us on the beautiful blue waters of Aruba, a tropical island in the Caribbean. We offer amazing yachting day tours and extended charters for visitors to Aruba. Whether it's a birthday party, a wedding reception, anniversary celebrations, graduation parties, or an office party, there is no better way to celebrate the occasion than by cruising along the calm waters of the island of Aruba. It’s time to turn your dreams of a yacht charter vacation into a reality with Exclusive Boat Aruba VIP.

Our beautiful Island Aruba, is located in the Caribbean Sea. The tropical climate is attractive for sailing, swimming, and snorkeling all year round as a destination for yacht charters, with an average temperature of 82°F and a gentle summer breeze. Just relax and let our crew take care of you.
